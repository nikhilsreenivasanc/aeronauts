Our Story

We are an organization at GEC Wayanad that provides students with the resources to develop drones.

Drone is the branch of technology that deals with the design, construction, operation, structural disposition, manufacture and application of robots.

Welcome to our college drone club website! Our club is dedicated to exploring the exciting world of drone technology and its endless possibilities. Our website is designed to be a hub for information, resources, and opportunities for members to connect with each other and share their passion for drones.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Provide an engaging and educational environment for those who are passionate about drone technology. Aims to facilitate learning opportunities, promote innovation, and encourage the safe and responsible use of drones. The goal is to empower students with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in the field of drone technology.

Vision

Envisions a future where drones are widely recognized as a valuable tool for a range of applications, from environmental monitoring and disaster relief to transportation and delivery. Believes that by fostering a community of drone enthusiasts and experts, we can help advance the field of drone technology and contribute to its growth and development. Our vision is to inspire the next generation of drone innovators and leaders.

GECW | Drone Club

Our goals are to facilitate learning opportunities, promote innovation,encourage the safe and responsible use of drones. We believe that by fostering a community of drone enthusiasts and experts,we can help advance the field of drone technology and contribute to its growth and development. Through hands-on activities, workshops,and field trips, students will learn about the different types of drones,their capabilities, and how they can be used in various industries such as agriculture, construction, photography, and more.In addition to technical skills, we will also focus on soft skills such as communication, teamwork,and problem-solving. Students will have opportunities to work in groups, present their ideas and projects, and receive feedback from their peers and instructors.
